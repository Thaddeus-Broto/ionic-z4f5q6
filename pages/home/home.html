Résoudre

This is a Markdown Calculator app. Markdown is a reduction of the product price, basing on the inability to sell it at the initial price or original selling price. In other words, markdown is the difference between the original retail price and the actual selling price.

Résoudre is a company that calculates the necessary markdown rates for products around the world. It specializes in calculating your product's markdown prices if said product experiences a surplus in production.
